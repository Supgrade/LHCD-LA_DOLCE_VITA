Motto Detail

"La vita è bella!"

Life is beautiful!

Explanation

This popular Italian phrase celebrates the beauty of life and embodies the Italian philosophy of finding joy in everyday moments. It gained international recognition through Roberto Benigni's 1997 film of the same name.

Origin

This phrase has been a staple in Italian culture for generations, but became particularly famous after the award-winning film "La vita è bella" (1997).

Usage

Used to express appreciation for life's pleasures or to encourage a positive outlook during difficult times.
